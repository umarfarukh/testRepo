Hi, Cognizant believes in a focused and continuous learning process that helps you seamlessly adapt from academic life to the professional world. This begins with a classroom-based Campus Associate Training Program (CATP) for the first 14 weeks, followed by an on-the-job Dovetail training program for the subsequent 8 months. These programs are aimed at helping you become a well-rounded IT professional in the first year itself.

The Campus Associate Training program includes modules that cover technology, process and behavioral skills. As part of the program, tests and quiz sessions are conducted during and at the end of every module. Your performance in these tests and quizzes determines your success in the CATP.

We expect you to closely follow the learning plan and the course material to make sure that you reach all the milestones within the stipulated time. You will be evaluated on the Dovetail program twice (Phase 1 and Phase 2) before your confirmation.

We are confident that you will shine bright in the CATP as well as the Dovetail program. Nevertheless, we encourage you to prepare well to successfully clear the 70% benchmark in the CATP and both phases of the Dovetail program within 12 months from the date of joining Cognizant. In the event you are not able to do so, please refer to the Completion Criteria Section.

Successful Completion of Training and Conditions for employment continuation


a. As one of the key conditions of the employment offer, you will need to undergo an initial technical training named as Campus Associate Training Program (“CATP”) for a stipulated time frame of 14 weeks. The continuation of your employment is conditional upon the successful completion (securing 70% in each of the modules) of the training assessments - part of the CATP to the reasonable satisfaction of the Company (“CATP completion”). Failure to clear the assessments in 3 consecutive attempts (1 Module test + 2 re-tests) will lead to the immediate termination of your employment with the Company.

b. You will become eligible for confirmation at the end of 12 months from the date of joining subject to the conditions below;

  • Satisfactory performance of your duties and obligations in a diligent and professional manner in accordance with the Company’s requirements from time to time; and
  • Successful clearance of the Dovetail assessments (securing 70% in both Phase-I & II) within 12 months and in not more than 2 (Two) attempts. Dovetail is a one year self-learning and assessment program that is aimed to develop structured technical competency for the trainees. Failure in clearing the Dovetail may also result in the immediate termination of your employment with the Company; and
  • Certificate from your supervisor (“Supervisor”) certifying that you are performing the duties and obligations in a satisfactory manner. Further, the Supervisor reserves the right to confirm / defer / refuse the confirmation

    • c. If the confirmation of employment is refused to you on the ground mentioned in section 2 (b) (i) above then you will be placed on a Performance Improvement Plan (“PIP”) for such period not more than two months which will be communicated to you by the Company. Any failure to attain the required performance standards during the PIP shall lead to immediate termination of your employment.

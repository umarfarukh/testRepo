How do I update my education details in case the details need to be corrected?

a. Log on to https://gsd/Human Resources/PeopleLine-IndiaHR/

b. Choose the following: Category- HR Systems; Type- PeopleSoft; Item– Education/Experience

c. Give the description under 'Summary' and 'Details'.

How do I update my experience details in case of a data mismatch?

a. Log on to https://gsd/Human Resources/PeopleLine-IndiaHR/

b. Choose the following: Category– HR Systems; Type– PeopleSoft; Item– Education/Experience

c. Give the description under 'Summary' and 'Details'.

What is the procedure to update my bank details?

Log on to https://peoplesoft/Human Capital Management/Self Service/Payroll and Compensation/Personal Bank Information

How do I update my seat location?

The seat number has to be updated in https://globalsms.cognizant.com by the supervisor who initiates this, on behalf of the associate.

What is the process of converting my temporary access card to a permanent one?

The temporary access card issued during induction is valid only for the first 15 days.

To get a permanent access card to the facility you are mapped to, log on to

https://Adminservices.cognizant.com/request service/access card

What are the steps for raising a GSD request?

a. Go to internet explorer.

b. Log in to https://gsd.

c. Raise tickets under NSS, Human Resources, Academy, GWFM, etc and choose the category.

Am I entitled to receive a Laptop and Blackberry connection?

a. All Senior Managers and above are entitled to receive Laptops and Mobile connections.

b. All Associate Directors and above are entitled to receive Home phone & broad band and All Blackberry connections in addition to the above.

c. All Directors and above are entitled to receive Blackberry devices, in addition to the above.

What is the procedure to enroll in the Cognizant Gym?

a. Gym facilities are available in only some of our offices.

b. Please get in touch with your respective HR- talent manager to check the gym facilities in your respective city.

c. Visit the Gym facility and meet the gym co-ordinator.

d. Register your details and collect the membership card.

What is the procedure to apply for a wah card?

The wah cards are distributed in a phased manner, in all locations every month. The new joiners would receive a mail on the distribution from the location HR team, within 2 to 3 months of joining.

How do I apply for a new SIM?

Contact the telecom service providers at your respective locations. The vendors have set up kiosks in all our offices near the cafeteria.

How do I apply for a VPN?

Please contact your location NSS team directly in your respective locations.

How do I get my laptop and blackberry if I’m eligible to avail these assets?

Please contact your location NSS team directly in your respective locations.

How do I declare tax investments to get tax benefit?

Please note that you can declare your investments on 'investment declaration' page.

You should declare and furnish the copy of investment made on 'investments actual' page.

Please access My pay (https://mypay.cognizant.com), click on 'MyUpdate' and navigate to 'Investment Declaration' and 'Investments Actual' pages.

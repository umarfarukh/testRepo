Approval & ESA:

1. Card statements are usually sent to associates directly at the beginning of the month and is due 25 days later.

2. The associate is required to clear any dues on the AMEX card in full.

3. An expense claim is to be submitted via ESA using the normal expense process (for subsistence, taxi’s etc.,) to recover the business related charges

4. Please ensure you upload the statement, and associated receipts, especially where there’s a currency conversion.

5. The associate must indicate the items which are not the company’s liability as ‘Personal’, and submit the claim.

6. Once approved, payment of the claim is made via the usual weekly paycycles on Tuesdays & Fridays.

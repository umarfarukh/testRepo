Voluntary Veteran Profile


For Cognizant’s Veterans Initiative, we are collecting data on US Military Veterans that have joined Cognizant. With your participation, we hope to strengthen our Veterans in Technology Initiative by creating networks, mentorships, training, and development opportunities for our veteran’s community at Cognizant.


Please note that disclosure of this information is completely voluntary, and refusing to provide it will not subject you to any adverse treatment. This information will be used to keep you informed about networking events and other employee engagement/recognition initiatives.

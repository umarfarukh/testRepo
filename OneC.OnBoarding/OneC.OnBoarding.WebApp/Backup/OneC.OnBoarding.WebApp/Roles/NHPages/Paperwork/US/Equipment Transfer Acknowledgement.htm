Equipment Transfer Acknowledgement

 

As of the contract closing date, Cognizant will acquire current ING-owned technology infrastructure and equipment. As a Cognizant employee, you will continue to use your current computer and desk phone. The Cognizant IT team will modify and update your equipment throughout your first two months of employment. Below is an outline of the changes and when we expect these changes to occur.


Day 1 (August 16, 2012)

  • You will experience no change in infrastructure or equipment, and will continue to access the same computer and desk phone
  • You will receive a unique employee ID and password that will allow you to access your new Cognizant mailbox using URL https://usmail.cognizant.com

Beginning August 22, 2012

  • The Cognizant IT team will work with you to update your existing computer with Cognizant software and install an email client that will allow you to access your Cognizant email directly from your desktop. You will continue to have access to the ING systems that are required for your day-to-day responsibilities.

  • The Cognizant IT team will also be updating all desk phones to Cisco IP phone systems. Some phone numbers may change; however, any changes in phone numbers will be communicated to affected employees before changes are made
  • All changes and updates to computer and phone equipment will be completed with minimal disruption to business operations. Further details and a schedule for these updates will be published the week of August 22

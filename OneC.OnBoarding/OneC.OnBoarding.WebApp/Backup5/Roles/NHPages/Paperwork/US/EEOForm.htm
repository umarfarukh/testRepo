Veteran - An individual who served in military for not less than 90 consecutive days during a national emergency declared in accordance with federal law or was discharged from military service for an estabilished service-connected disability;was honorably discharged from military service; and is competent.

Disabled Veteran - A "disabled veteran" is (1) A veteran of the U.S. military,ground,naval or air service who is entitled to compensation(or who but for the receipt of military pay would be entitled to compensation) under laws administered by the Secretary of Veteran Affairs, or (2) A person who was discharged or released from active duty because of a service-connected disability.

Armed Forces Service Medal Veteran - An "Armed Forces Service Medal veteran" is any veteran who, while serving on active duty in the U.S. military,ground,naval or air service,participated in a United States military operation for which an Armed Forces service medal was awarded pursuant to Executive Order 12985.The Affirmative Action team can request a copy of the DD Form 214 otherwise known as their Certificate of Release or Discharge from Active Duty to determine what veteran preferences you are eligible for as a veteran.Ex:Vietanam Era,Korean,Desert Shield,Operational Iraqi Freedom. A full list of the eligible campaigns can be found at:http://www.opm.gov/staffingportal/vgmedal2.asp

Other Protected Veteran - An "other protected veteran" is a veteran who served on active duty in the U.S. military, ground,naval or air service during a war or in a campaign or expeditionfor which a campaign badge has been authorized ,under the laws administered by the Department of Defense. http://www.opm.gov/staffingportal/uniserv.asp

Recently Seperated Veteran - "Recently seperated veteran" is any veteran during the three-year period beginning on the date of such veteran's discharge or release from the active duty in the U.S. military,ground,naval or air service.

Reservists/National Guard -is an individual that is currently serving in the National Guard or any branch of the military reserves.

An Individual with a Disability - An "individual with a disability" is defined as a person who (1) has a physical or mental impairment which substantially limits one or more of his or her major life activities,(2) has a record of such impairment , or (3) is regarded as having such an impairment.

Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA)
The employer also is subject to the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA).Consistent with the ADA,Cognizant's policy is to provide reasonable accomodations to any individual with a disability who needs such an accomodation to complete the job application process or to perform the job in question.If you need such an accomodation, you may request it any time by contacting your Talent Manager or your immediate supervisor.Making a request for an accomodation will not subject you to any adversr treatment.

If you have a disability or are a covered veteran and would like to self-identify ,please complete this form and return to Cognizant Human Resource Benefits,either along with your initial new hire paperwork,or email the document to Cognizant EEO and Affirmative Action Team at AAHelp@cognizant.com.

PAYCHECK MAILING CONSENT FORM

As an employee of Cognizant, you will be offered the benefit of having your paycheck directly deposited into one or two bank accounts. To set-up a direct deposit account(s), you must complete your information in the HR system (HCM) once you commence employment.

To ensure your direct deposit information is accurate, Cognizant’s payroll team will run a pre-notification on your account(s). While the pre-notification is completed, your first few paychecks may be actual checks. Similarly, if at any time you decide to change your bank account information, a pre-notification will be run, and you will receive an actual paycheck until your account information has been verified. In both of the instances, the actual paycheck will be mailed to the home address you have provided during onboarding and maintain in HCM.

By signing this document below, you agree to the mailing of any actual paycheck to the home address listed in the HCM.

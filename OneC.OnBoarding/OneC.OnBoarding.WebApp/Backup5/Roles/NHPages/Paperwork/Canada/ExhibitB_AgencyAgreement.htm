By this Agreement:

1.

Engagement. Cognizant Canada or one of its affiliates (collectively with Cognizant Canada, “Cognizant”), in accordance with the terms of the Agency Agreement (the “Agency Agreement”), dated , by and between Cognizant and (the “Agency”), hereby engages the Consultant to provide certain consulting and other services as requested by Cognizant (the “Work”).

2.

Compensation. In full consideration of all services rendered by the Consultant and for all rights granted or relinquished by the Consultant under this Agreement, Cognizant shall pay certain fees to Agency in accordance with the terms of the Agency Agreement, and Agency shall pay the Consultant pursuant to an existing agreement between Agency and the Consultant. 3.

Non-Solicitation/Non-Compete. During the period of time during which the Consultant performs services for Cognizant and for one (1) year thereafter, Consultant will not directly or indirectly:

(a) Compete with Cognizant by soliciting or accepting any engagement with a Cognizant Client other than through Cognizant.
(b) Hire or assist in soliciting or hiring, any employee or consultant working for Cognizant or cause any such employee or consultant to leave the services of Cognizant, assist such employees or consultant to take up employment with a Cognizant Client, a competitor of Cognizant or any other entity or person or attempt to do any of the foregoing.
For purposes of this Agreement, “Cognizant Client” shall mean any person or entity to which Consultant would be introduced to or would be reporting to, directly or indirectly, for purposes of an assignment hereunder.
In the event of an actual or threatened breach by Consultant of any of the provisions of this Section 3, Consultant agrees that Cognizant’s remedy at law will be inadequate, and accordingly, Cognizant shall be entitled to injunctive relief in any action or proceeding brought to enforce the terms of this Section 3.

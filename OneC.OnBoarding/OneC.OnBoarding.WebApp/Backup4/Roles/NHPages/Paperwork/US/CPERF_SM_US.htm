Maintenance and support

Cognizant issued Smartphone devices are purchased with a 2 year contract with the wireless carrier. It is the responsibility of the employee to ensure proper use and care of the device during the 2 year contract. During the 1st year of the contract, if the device is not functioning at 100% and it is determined that the device needs to be replaced (no physical or liquid damage), the employee will be provided with a “Warranty Replacement” for the same device. During the 2nd year of the contract, if the device is not functioning at 100% and it is determined that the device needs to be replaced; it is the financial responsibility of the employee.

Employee Transfer, Resignation or Termination

In the case of a resignation, termination, or transfer to a new geography:
- Corporate purchased devices must be returned back to the company
- If you leave the company or transfer to new geography and you had paid for the device, you can choose to either
move the phone number to your personal account (with the same wireless carrier) and take the device with you or
pay the early termination fee on the line and take the device with you or return the equipment back to Cognizant
with no refund.
- Porting requests must be submitted via a GSD ticket at least 5 days in advance of the termination date.

Welcome to Cognizant




Dear



I am delighted that you will soon be joining the Cognizant UKI family. We continue to deliver strong business growth and provide our associates with a great opportunity to build their careers in a dynamic environment.


Our brand grows along with our client base year on year; we work with many well-known blue chip global companies and leading UKI businesses.


The UK, Ireland & Jersey markets are of strategic importance to Cognizant and you are joining a team of associates that is the key to providing exceptional client service and industry leading growth.


The world around us is changing fast and with change comes opportunity. At Cognizant, we have the passion, ambition and commitment to enable and lead this change for our clients. Enabling and leading this change externally requires us to do the same internally – to think and act differently, while retaining customer centricity as a defining element of our culture. You are joining a team that’s on a mission to relentlessly challenge the status quo internally and externally in order to deliver higher levels of value to our clients. This moves us from an “offshore outsourcing services leader” to a “global services leader”. Put simply, Tier One. Period.


You will shortly be invited to a corporate induction program, where you will have the opportunity to hear more about the company,meet some of the people who lead our enabling functions and have a great opportunity to start building your internal network.


We are on an exciting journey with much to do, but we also do not forget about our responsibility to society and the communities we work in, through a growing Outreach Programme, which I hope you will want to get involved with once you have settled in.



Welcome aboard and I look forward to meeting and working with you.




Vivek K. Daga

Vice President and Country Manager, UKI

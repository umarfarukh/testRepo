June 18, 2012


Re: Amendment to Offer of Employment Letter with Cognizant Technology Solutions Services, LLC (“Cognizant”)



1. As stated during the Cognizant Town Hall Meeting on Friday June 15, 2012, we are amending your offer of employment letter with Cognizant, dated June 15, 2012 (the “Offer Letter”) with respect to your paid time off. The following shall amend and replace in its entirety the “Vacation” paragraph in your Offer Letter.


PAID TIME OFF: You will remain on a modified version of the ING U.S. Paid Time Off (“PTO”) policy with effect from your hire date with Cognizant. However, your annual Paid Time Off allotment will be fixed and will not grow in the future based on your tenure. Your annual PTO allotment with Cognizant will be calculated using your tenure as of the end of the year 2013, with effect from your date of hire with Cognizant. This allotment may be equal to or greater than your current annual PTO allotment depending on your service date with ING U.S. You will not receive any adjustment to your base salary as a result of this arrangement. Your PTO allotment will include vacation, sick time and personal days

  1. If you are an employee based in North Dakota, you will begin to accrue PTO with Cognizant with effect from October 1, 2012. If you are an employee based in any other state, you will begin to accrue PTO with Cognizant with effect from September 1, 2012.

  2. Full-time and part-time regular employees are eligible for PTO. Part-time employees receive a prorated PTO allotment based on their scheduled hours.

  3. Except for employees in North Dakota, your PTO allotment is provided monthly at 1/12 of your annual allotment. PTO may be used prior to earning it as long as use does not exceed the maximum for the year. For employees in North Dakota, your PTO allotment is provided incrementally throughout the year on a quarterly basis. You will receive 25% of your allotment on the first day of each new quarter. PTO is not available for use until it is allotted to you.

  4. If you increase or decrease your scheduled hours, your PTO allotment for the year will be adjusted at that time. Short-term changes for less than three months or changes in the fourth quarter do not require an adjustment to your PTO allotment. Full-time employees may carry over a maximum of 40 hours into the next calendar year. The maximum carryover for part-time employees shall be prorated based on the number of hours you work in a workweek. Carryover PTO hours must be used before you tap into your new allotment of PTO.

  5. Please refer to the examples below as an illustration of the arrangement

  1. Example 1: Your hire date with ING U.S. is March 1, 2007. You have completed 5 years of service with ING U.S. and your current PTO allotment is 184 hours. Your annual PTO allotment with Cognizant will remain 184 hours.

  2. Example 2: Your hire date with ING U.S. is March 1, 2008. Your current PTO allotment is 160 hours. You will complete 5 years of service with ING U.S. on March 1, 2013 and would have received an additional 24 hours of PTO on January 1, 2013 per the ING U.S. PTO policy. Your annual PTO allotment with Cognizant will be 184 hours and it becomes effective immediately.

The terms and conditions of your employment pursuant to this letter, the Amendment and NDA may not be changed except as otherwise expressly specified in this letter, the Amendment or in the NDA.

Other than as expressly amended in Paragraphs 1 and 2 above, all other terms and conditions of the Offer Letter remain valid
